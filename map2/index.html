Map of goan churches

Data Sources: http://goanchurches.info , http://www.archgoadaman.org & googlemaps

On a Mobile Phone, for best results open the map using google maps by clicking on the icon on the top right corner.

All Deaneries are colour coded. However, since google maps only allows about 7 colours & we have 19 deaneries, colours had to be repeated 3 times. You can use the filter to drill down.
